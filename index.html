About Me

The Engineer Behind the Brand

I'm Damarcus Lett — a Business Information Systems graduate turned Technical Program Manager at Microsoft, where I drive Xbox engineering initiatives that reach millions of users worldwide.

My journey spans General Electric, Emerson Climate Technologies, Johnson & Johnson, Boeing, Dell Technologies, Intel, and now Microsoft. Each stop sharpened my ability to bridge complex technical systems with strategic business outcomes.

Now I'm on a mission to demystify tech and engineering for the next generation — sharing real stories from inside the world's most iconic companies.
